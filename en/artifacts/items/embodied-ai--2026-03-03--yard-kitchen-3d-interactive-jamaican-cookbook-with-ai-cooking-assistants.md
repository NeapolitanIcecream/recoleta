# Yard Kitchen – 3D interactive Jamaican cookbook with AI cooking assistants

## Summary
Yard Kitchen is a 3D interactive digital cookbook focused on traditional Jamaican recipes, combining two AI cooking assistants to provide context-aware guidance while cooking. Its core value lies in bringing together cultural preservation, an immersive reading experience, and AI Q&A within a single cooking platform.

## Problem
- Traditional cookbooks often provide only static text, making it difficult to offer immediate, situational guidance during cooking.
- Traditional Jamaican recipes and cooking knowledge have preservation and transmission needs, especially content that includes cultural background and experiential techniques.
- Ordinary digital recipe products have limited interactivity, making it hard to balance learning experience, cultural storytelling, and practical assistance at the same time.

## Approach
- Build a **3D interactive cookbook** that offers an immersive reading experience with page-turn animations, ambient sound effects, and more.
- Include **14 traditional Jamaican recipes**, each with historical background, ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and plating guidance.
- Provide two **AI cooking assistants**: Granny Miss Vie focuses on traditional wisdom, while Chef Marcus focuses on professional cooking techniques.
- The AI assistants support **voice-enabled** and **context-aware** interactions. Put simply, users can ask questions directly while cooking, and the system gives suggestions based on the current recipe content.
