# Show HN: AI Resume Chatbot – recruiters can chat with your resume

## Summary
This is an AI resume platform for recruiting scenarios: after candidates upload their resumes, the system uses AI to parse them and enables recruiters to discover candidates through semantic search. It also optionally provides a “resume chatbot” that recruiters can talk to instantly. Its core value is turning a static PDF resume into a dynamic talent profile that is searchable, shareable, and interactive.

## Problem
- Traditional PDF resumes are static documents that are easy to forget, and recruiters can only view/download them without asking clarifying questions in real time.
- Keyword search can easily miss strong candidates because real experience is often described in different ways; for example, “scaled teams” and “grew org from 3 to 40” are semantically related but lexically different.
- There are also visibility and collaboration issues in recruiting workflows: candidates need control over who can see their profiles, and recruiting teams need shared candidate pools to reduce duplicate sourcing.

## Approach
- After candidates upload PDF resumes, the system automatically extracts experience, skills, and education information to generate structured profiles that can be public or shared selectively.
- Recruiters use natural language for AI-driven semantic search, and the system matches candidates based on the meaning of “what they have actually done,” not just keywords.
- Recruiting teams can build shared candidate pools and collect candidate resumes directly into the organization’s private talent database through dedicated upload links.
- After a paid upgrade, a candidate’s profile includes a real-time AI chatbot, allowing recruiters to ask questions directly about the resume content and get instant answers; candidates receive notifications and can join at any time.
