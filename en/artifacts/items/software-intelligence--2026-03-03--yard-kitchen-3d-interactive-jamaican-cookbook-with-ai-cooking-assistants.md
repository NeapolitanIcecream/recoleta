# Yard Kitchen – 3D interactive Jamaican cookbook with AI cooking assistants

## Summary
This is a 3D interactive digital cookbook product focused on traditional Jamaican recipes, rather than a typical academic paper. It combines 14 heritage recipes, an immersive page-flipping reading experience, and two conversational AI cooking assistants to provide more vivid cooking guidance.

## Problem
- Traditional cookbooks are often static text and lack real-time guidance, technique explanations, and cultural context during the cooking process.
- Ordinary digital cookbooks are relatively weak in interactivity, making it hard to provide a more immersive and easier-to-understand support experience while actually cooking.
- Old-school Jamaican recipes and cooking knowledge have cultural preservation value; without a more accessible digital format, their transmission is limited.

## Approach
- Build a **3D interactive cookbook** that enhances the reading and usage experience with realistic page-turn animations and ambient sound effects.
- Digitally organize 14 traditional Jamaican recipes, with each dish including history, ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and plating guidance.
- Introduce two **AI cooking assistants**: Granny Miss Vie provides traditional knowledge, and Chef Marcus provides professional cooking techniques.
- The assistants support voice and context-aware Q&A, aiming to provide more natural, companion-like guidance during cooking.

## Results
- The product claims to include **14** traditional Jamaican recipes, of which **2** can be previewed for free.
- It offers **2** AI assistant personas: **Miss Vie** and **Chef Marcus**, and claims to provide **unlimited AI chat** in paid plans.
- The text **does not provide academic experiments, benchmark tests, or quantitative performance metrics**, nor does it make a formal comparison with other digital cookbooks or AI cooking systems.
- The strongest concrete product claims are: a 3D page-flipping experience, voice/context-aware assistants, step-by-step cooking guidance, and practical help such as seasoning and heat-control advice mentioned in user reviews.
