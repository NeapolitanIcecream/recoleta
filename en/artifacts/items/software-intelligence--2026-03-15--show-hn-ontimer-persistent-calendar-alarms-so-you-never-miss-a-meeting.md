# Show HN: OnTimer – persistent calendar alarms so you never miss a meeting

## Summary
OnTimer is an iPhone calendar reminder app that turns ordinary, easy-to-miss calendar notifications into loud, persistent alarms to reduce missed meetings and lateness. It is aimed at people who rely on their schedules but are often "under-reminded" by passive notifications, and it supports integration with multiple Google and Microsoft calendars.

## Problem
- The problem it aims to solve is: **standard calendar reminders are too passive and too easy to swipe away or ignore**, causing users to miss meetings, calls, and appointments.
- This matters because for busy professionals, people with back-to-back meetings, and those with time blindness or ADHD tendencies, **missing meetings directly affects work reliability and punctual attendance**.
- Existing calendar apps mainly "show notifications" rather than **forcefully capturing attention and prompting action** at critical moments.

## Approach
- The core mechanism is simple: **after connecting the user's calendar, it automatically prepares stronger reminders for upcoming events, upgrading event reminders from ordinary notifications to persistent alarms**.
- These reminders trigger before meetings, calls, or appointments, and **stay loud and visible until the user actively dismisses them**, making them harder to miss than system notifications.
- The app supports **Google and Microsoft** calendars, as well as **multiple calendars/multiple accounts**, with no need to manually create reminders for each event.
- For events with a location, it offers the **Time To Leave** feature, which reminds users when to leave based on **travel time and traffic conditions** (a paid feature).
