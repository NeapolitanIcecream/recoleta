# Show HN: OnTimer – persistent calendar alarms so you never miss a meeting

## Summary
OnTimer is an iPhone calendar reminder app that turns ordinary, easy-to-miss calendar notifications into louder, persistently visible alarms to reduce missed meetings and lateness. It is aimed at users who rely on their schedules but often miss reminders, and it supports multiple Google and Microsoft calendars.

## Problem
- The problem it solves is that standard calendar reminders on iPhone are usually passive notifications that appear briefly and are easy to swipe away or ignore, causing users to miss meetings, calls, or appointments.
- This matters because for busy professionals, people with back-to-back meetings, and those with time blindness or ADHD tendencies, missing reminders can directly lead to lateness, no-shows, and work disruption.
- Existing calendar apps are more oriented toward “displaying notifications” than truly capturing the user’s attention at critical moments.

## Approach
- The core mechanism is simple: connect the user’s Google or Microsoft calendar, automatically monitor upcoming events, and generate reminder alarms for those events in advance.
- Unlike ordinary notifications, OnTimer triggers reminders before an event that are “loud, persistent, and require manual dismissal,” making them harder to ignore.
- For events with a location, it also provides Time To Leave departure alerts based on travel time and traffic conditions to help users judge when they should leave; this is a paid feature.
- It does not replace the existing calendar, but adds a stronger reminder execution layer on top of the current schedule, while supporting unified access to multiple calendar accounts.
