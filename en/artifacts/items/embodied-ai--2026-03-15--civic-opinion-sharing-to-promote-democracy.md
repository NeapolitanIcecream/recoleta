# Civic opinion sharing to promote democracy

## Summary
OurPublicVoice is a nonpartisan civic engagement platform that allows the public to express positions on policy issues and view real-time anonymous comparisons from the community level to the national level. Its core value is to use more accessible, data-driven expressions of public opinion to narrow the gap between public sentiment and legislative action.

## Problem
- The problem it aims to solve is that the public lacks a low-barrier, structured, and comparable channel for expressing views on policy issues from the local to the federal level.
- This matters because if democratic decision-making cannot reflect public opinion in a timely and clear way, it leads to a disconnect between public sentiment and legislative action.
- Existing forms of informational participation are often fragmented and opaque, making it difficult for citizens to understand how their views differ from those of their community, state, and the nation.

## Approach
- It provides nonpartisan summaries of policy issues, helping users quickly understand the issue before taking a position.
- Users can vote on each issue as support, oppose, or neutral, using the simplest three-way classification to collect opinions.
- The platform displays real-time, anonymous demographic breakdowns at different levels such as community, state, and nation, helping users compare views across different groups and regions.
- It supports filtering active issues by federal, state, county, city, and geographic location, and allows users to register accounts to continuously track issues they care about.
