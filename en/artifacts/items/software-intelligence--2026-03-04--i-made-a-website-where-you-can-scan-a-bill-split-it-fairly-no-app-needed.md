# I made a website where you can: Scan a bill. Split it fairly. No app needed

## Summary
SnapFair is a web tool that requires no app installation, designed to scan a bill and split expenses fairly among multiple people. It aims to simplify the manual calculation, item-by-item allocation, and payment-request process involved in settling restaurant bills into a lightweight, shareable experience.

## Problem
- It addresses the tedious and error-prone problem of **bill scanning, item allocation, and fair expense splitting** after group dining.
- Traditional methods often require manual entry, mental math for splitting the bill, or forcing everyone to install the same app, creating a high barrier to use.
- This problem matters because it is a frequent real-life scenario that affects payment efficiency, social experience, and collection accuracy.

## Approach
- Use a mobile web page to **scan a bill** directly, with no need to download an app.
- The system first extracts items and prices from the bill, then lets users **edit names or prices and remove recognition mistakes**, indicating a workflow of “automatic recognition + manual correction.”
- Users can **add everyone at the table** and assign each dish/item to the corresponding person by tapping on people.
- It supports **sharing the split result** and displays “someone shared a bill split with you / someone is requesting payment,” indicating that it also covers the sharing and payment-request steps.
