Its core value is to provide benchmark-based guidance for content teams choosing a more reliable large model.

Problem

  • The problem the article addresses is: in ecommerce content production, how to determine which large model is better suited to generating more accurate, more trustworthy, and more reliably cited E-E-A-T content.
  • This matters because if ecommerce content contains factual errors, fake citations, or hallucinations, it will directly damage user trust, SEO performance, and commercial conversion.
  • Existing models are often marketed as stronger, but differences in accuracy, citation reliability, and hallucination control require systematic benchmarking to assess.

Approach
