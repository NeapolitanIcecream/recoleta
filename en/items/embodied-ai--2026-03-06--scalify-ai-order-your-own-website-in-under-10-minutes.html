Summary

Problem

  • The “problem” it addresses is helping users place an order and obtain a website in a short amount of time, rather than a technical challenge in scientific research.
  • Its importance lies in the efficiency and convenience of commercial website delivery, but it has no direct relation to research on robot foundation models, vision-language-action, or world models.

Approach
